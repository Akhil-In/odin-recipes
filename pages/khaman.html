KHAMAN RECIPE

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon refined oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 15 curry leaves
  • 4 sliced green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon coconut powder
  • 1 handful coriander leaves

List of Instruction to be followed To Prepare Samosa

  1. Prepare the Dhokla batter and let it ferment for 1-2 hours.
  2. Cook the batter for 15-20 minutes in the idli/dhokla cooker
  3. Prepare the tempering
  4. Serve the moist Khaman Dhokla with Faafda and Jalebi
