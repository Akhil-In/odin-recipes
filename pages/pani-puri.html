Panipuri-Recipe

Items Needed to Prepare Pani-Puri

  1. Maida
  2. Any Refined-Oil
  3. Jeera(Cumin)
  4. Chilli
  5. Salt (White or Black)
  6. Garlic
  7. Ginger
  8. Water
  9. Potato
  10. White Pea
  11. Gram
  12. Tomato
  13. Pani-Puri Spices

List of Instruction to be followed To Prepare Pani-Puri

  1. Take Maida about 300gm and paur some water in it. Mix it until it's convert into dough
  2. Make a Small Roll out of Dough maida
  3. Make a small chip of rolled maida with Wooden Roller
  4. Take Oil abou 500ml an pour into bowl and heat it
  5. Take small chips of maida and carefully dop into oil pan to get round crispy puri
  6. Take potato and boil it until it gets Soft.
  7. After that take out your boile potato and mix with Spices, Gram and Peas
  8. You Pani - Puri Recipe is Ready to serve
