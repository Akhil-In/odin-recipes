Gujhiya RECIPE

Items Needed to Prepare Gujhiya

  1. 3 cup all purpose flour
  2. 300 gm khoya
  3. 1 1/2 cup sugar
  4. 1 tablespoon powdered green cardamom
  5. 2 tablespoon finely chopped almonds
  6. 1/4 cup semolina
  7. 1 1/2 cup ghee
  8. 1 1/2 cup water

List of Instruction to be followed To Prepare Gujhiya

  1. Mix together all purpose flour and water
  2. Knead a soft dough
  3. Prepare the filling for Gujiya
  4. Prepare puris and fill them with the stuffing
  5. Deep fry the Gujiya and enjoy
